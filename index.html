Turtle

Some cool facts about turtles:

  1. All turtles lay eggs on the land.
  2. There are over 300 species of turtles, tortoises, and terrapins combined!
  3. Some tortoises can live for up to 150 years!
  4. There are three types of turtles: turtles, tortoises, and terrapins.

Bird

Some cool facts about birds:

  1. All modern birds evolved from dinosaurs with wings!
  2. There are about 10000 different kinds of species of birds.
  3. The bird that has the longest lifespan is the african grey.
  4. Birds can live anywhere from 1-100 years!
  5. Birds are one of the most common pets.

Emu

Some cool facts about emus:

  • Emus can jump 7 feet in the air!
  • Emus has two eyelids. One eyelid is used for blinking, while the other prevents dust and sand to enter the eyes.
  • Emus have two feathers per follicle, unlike most animals which only have 1 per follicle.
  • The emu can stride 9 feet (or 2.7 meters) if they are running.
